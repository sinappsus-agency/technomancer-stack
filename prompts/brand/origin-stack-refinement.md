# Origin Stack Refinement Prompt

**Chapter Reference:** Chapter 1 — The Origin Stack
**Usage:** Paste this into Claude, GPT-4, or your preferred model. Fill in the [BRACKETS] with your own information before submitting.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are helping me build an Origin Stack — the foundational architecture of my personal brand. This is not marketing copy. It is a precision diagnostic of what genuinely positions me and why it should matter to the specific people I most want to reach.

Here is my raw material:

**My background:**
[Describe your career path, key turning points, industries, roles, years of experience]

**What I do right now:**
[Describe your current offer, who you serve, what they hire you for]

**What I believe that people in my space often get wrong:**
[Your contrarian or distinctive belief about your field]

**The transformation I create for clients:**
[Before state → after state. Be specific and concrete, not abstract]

**My unfair advantages:**
[What you know, have experienced, or can access that others in your space typically cannot]

Using this material, help me refine my Origin Stack across these four layers:

1. **Mission** — A single sentence that states what you exist to do and why it matters. Should be specific enough to exclude the wrong audience. Not a tagline — an orientation statement.

2. **Method** — A brief description of your distinctive approach. What makes how you work different from the industry default? What is your underlying philosophy?

3. **Moment** — The specific insight, experience, or realisation that changed how you see your field. This is the hinge point of your story.

4. **Manifesto** — Three to five deeply-held beliefs about your field, your clients, or what real excellence requires. These are your conviction statements.

After drafting each layer, identify any areas where the language is generic (could belong to anyone in your space) and suggest a more specific alternative.

Then give me your honest assessment: which layer feels the most authentic and specific, and which layer is still the most underdeveloped?
```

---

## How to Use This Output

1. Review each layer for generic language — replace it with language that could only be yours
2. Read the Moment aloud — if it does not sound like a real story, it probably is not fully formed yet
3. The Manifesto is your content fuel — each belief statement is a potential post, essay, or episode
4. Run the full Origin Stack through the Narrative Systems framework (Chapter 2) to map it to your platform content

---

## Iteration Note

This prompt is designed to be run more than once. After your first pass, copy the output back in with the instruction: "Now be more specific. Replace any phrase that could belong to any [your profession] with something that could only belong to me." Repeat until the language is precise.
